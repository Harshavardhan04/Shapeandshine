Our Services

HR Policies & Procedures: set-up and implementation

Having robust HR policies and procedures in place is a building block for any successful organisation. We will not only draft these policies and procedures for you, but will also help you in implementing them, monitoring them, and providing related advice and training to managers and employees, all whilst ensuring that they are embedded in the company’s culture.

Employee work-life cycle management

There is a saying that the first impression is the last impression. We not only believe in this, but will ensure to make the journey of employees in your organisation memorable and positive by providing smooth onboarding, supporting them during their stay in the company as and when required, and maintaining all records as per legal requirements from onboarding till exit.

HR Compliance

It’s the commitment of your business to follow working standards set out by Indian labour laws. We will look after all your compliance needs. Failing to meet your legal obligations is unlawful and can lead to industrial disputes or employment claims. By handing over these responsibilities to us, you can have peaceful nights of sleep.

Performance Management

If you’re looking to improve the turnover, productivity and morale of your organisation, performance management is an essential tool that should be well integrated within your business. Our HR consultants will work with you to design, implement and monitor clear performance management strategies that are well suited and specific for your company.

Benefits strategy and administration

We will help you to design and implement policies and processes which your organisations can use to pay and reward employees’ contributions to the organisation’s success. These benefit administration strategies will also enable you to retain a talented workforce hence reducing high labour turnover. We will also maintain all related records.

Organisational Structure

In any organisation, it’s the people (employees and clients) who always remain in the centre with everything revolving around them. Having the right structure in place not only helps the business to grow smoothly, but it also helps in growth and development of all employees. We will work with you to put the right structure in place.

Succession Planning

We will work with you to establish the most appropriate and effective succession strategy which can help your businesses in its future growth plans and in retaining key staff to drive productivity to new heights.

Recruitment

Our consultants, with expertise of working with people from diverse backgrounds, countries and solid knowledge of different business domains, can handle any recruitment challenges and add value in this area. We can help you in finding a suitable candidate for any position in any industry. Campus hiring, taking the first round of interviews, creating a talent pipeline, writing JDs and job adverts, conducting job grading, reference checks etc are just some of the services included in this package.

Employee Training & Development

We can work with you to conduct training-needs analysis, prepare departmental or individual development plans, and design and deliver bespoke soft-skills and HR related training to employees and managers. We also have expertise in delivering role based soft-skills and HR training to shop floor staff, front line staff and managers/supervisors.

Employee Relations

Our consultants can help you to deal with any day to day employee relations issues of all complexity levels.

People analytics, employee management and well-being are also our consultants' areas of interest and we are well equipped to help our clients in these areas as well. Our approach will vary greatly depending on your company’s nature and current situation.
